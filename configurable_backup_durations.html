Configurable backup durations 


Configurable backup durations will allow you to setup scenarios like

"I want to keep hourly backups for the past day, weekly backups for the past 6 months, and monthly backups until capacity"

And for that scenario it would keep backups, hourly weekly and monthly as you want, and it would keep monthly backups until the drive was full. 

Or, if you don't want to use the entire drive for backups,

"I want to keep hourly backups for the past day, daily backups for the past month, and monthly backups for the past 6 months"

And in that scenario, it would keep hourly daily and monthly backups, and would delete the monthly backups after 6 months.


Exactly _how_ you will configure this has not yet been established.
